D.Watson is a Pakistani retail pharmacy chain founded in 1975 by brothers Zahid and Zafar Iqbal Bakhtawari in Islamabad. The company was named in tribute to the English doctor, Dr. Watson, who helped Zafar Iqbal with an eye ailment that almost caused him to lose his sight.
